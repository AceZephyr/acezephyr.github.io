So, you want to compete.

That's great! But there are some things you should know first.


Where do I find out about where competitions are?

You can find a list of all of them at the World Cubing Association's Competitions page.

You can also filter the very long list of competitions by continent or country with the "Region" selector.


I've found a competition, how do I sign up for it?

There are two main methods of signing up for a competition in the USA, and competitions will probably use one or the other.

1) CubingUSA:

When you've found the competition, click the website link. Use the following instructions if the page has a blue header with the competition name.

Go under the "Registration" tab and fill out all the information you can. If this is your first competition, skip the WCA ID.

Make sure to fill in all the events you will be signed up for, in case there's a fee to participate in it.

You will then get a PayPal link to pay the registration fee. You will probably have to pay this before you are signed up.

2) WCA registration system

This is a newer system, but still fairly difficult to register with. If the competition website link just opens the same page, use this.

Click the "Register" tab off to the side or above. Follow the instructions until you have registered.

To pay, go to the Registration Payment and Information tab. The PayPal link should be there.
