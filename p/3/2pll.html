2-look PLL

What is 2-look PLL?

PLL (Permutation of the Last Layer) is the last step of the CFOP method, where you move all the pieces around the top layer.

2-look PLL solves this in two steps, and there are only 6 algorithms total.

If you want to get faster after being fairly good at beginner's method PLL, this is what to learn.
