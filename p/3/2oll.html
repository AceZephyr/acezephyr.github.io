2-look OLL

What is 2-look OLL?

OLL (Orientation of the Last Layer) is the third step of the CFOP method, where you orient all the pieces on the last layer.

2-look OLL solves this in two steps, and there are only 10 algorithms total.

If you want to get faster after being fairly good at beginner's method OLL, this is what to learn.
