What is OLL?

OLL (Orientation of the Last Layer) is the third step of the CFOP method, where you orient all the pieces on the last layer.

There are 57 cases. If you have not yet learned 2-look OLL, learn that first.

The cases are divided up into shapes the already oriented pieces make. Links to each group are below.

The Cross OLLs can be accessed by clicking the Cross link below.
