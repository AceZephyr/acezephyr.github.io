Where I get my algs from and other places to check out:

The Speedsolving Wiki (OLL, PLL) is a good resource, and can be edited freely.

Algdb.net has an incredible amount of 3x3 stuff, and it can be added to.

cyotheking.com has a bunch of 3x3 and 2x2 stuff.

Sarah Strong's website also has a bunch of 3x3, 5x5, minx, squan, and skewb stuff, including a guide to her skewb method.
